Devnet Company Group Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Devnet Company Group collects, uses, discloses, and protects the personal information of individuals.

Information We Collect

1. We may collect various types of personal information about you, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address.
  • Professional Information: Job title, company name, industry.
  • Inquiry Information: Information you provide when you contact us with inquiries or requests (e.g., through our website, email, or phone).
  • Other Information: Any other information you voluntarily provide to us.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information through various means, including:

  • Directly from you: When you provide information to us through our website, by email, phone, or in person.
  • When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information using technologies such as cookies

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for various purposes, including:

  • To respond to your inquiries and fulfill your requests.
  • To provide you with information about our services.
  • To communicate with you about our company and our services.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your personal information to:

  • Our affiliates and subsidiaries.
  • Service providers who assist us in providing our services (e.g., website hosting providers, email marketing providers).
  • Business partners with whom we collaborate.
  • Third parties, where required, with your consent. We will not pass on your personal information to third parties for any other purpose unless you have consented to us to do so or it is permitted by law.
